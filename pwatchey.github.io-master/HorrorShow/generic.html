Bio

My name is Paige.
I adore everything horror, art, music, makeup, and photography related. This is what spawned "The Horror Show."
"The Horror Show" is a place where everything I live and breathe can gather and live. If you don't like it, then what are you doing here?

About the "The Horror Show Blog"

"The Horror Show" is a way for me to collect all my thoughts and wonderings in one place instead of several misshapen journals, small flash drives and random pieces of paper.

The opinions, views, and thoughts in this blog are mine and mine alone. They do not reflect the views of the people involved with the movies, music, etc. If I do get something wrong (facts and whatnot) please feel free to tell me.
"The Horror Show" is also home to the music I am making with the few friends I have. We are hoping to share this music with you and the world soon. So watch out.
As always, please use Viewer Discretion when using this site. It may get pretty dark and disgusting. But who knows.
